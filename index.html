Frequently asked questions

What is Comparizzon?

Comparizzon is an anonymous life-comparison tool that shows how your answers about your looks, personality, lifestyle, dating life, work, money, habits, and health stack up against thousands of real people across 18 categories. No account, no email, free to start.

How does Comparizzon work?

Pick any category and start answering. For every question you'll see live percentages showing how common or rare your answer is. At the end, the overview gives you a uniqueness score from 0 to 100, per-category breakdowns, and a downloadable PDF report.

How long does the questionnaire take?

About 5 to 10 minutes for the full 200-question set — but even 30 well-answered questions are enough to see meaningful comparisons. Skip anything you don't want to answer.

Is Comparizzon free?

Yes. Answering and seeing live percentages is free. A one-time €1 payment unlocks your full overview — uniqueness score, every category breakdown, age/gender/country filters, and the PDF report. There is no subscription and no renewal.

Do I need an account or email?

No. Comparizzon never asks for your name, email, phone number, or social login. Your progress is saved locally in your browser. Only the €1 unlock uses an email — purely so you can restore access on another device.

Is my data anonymous and private?

Yes. Comparizzon stores only anonymous, aggregated answers. Payments are handled securely by Stripe so we never see your card details, and nothing is sold or shared with advertisers.

What is the uniqueness score?

A number from 0 to 100 that reflects how rare your overall mix of answers is. Near 100 = statistically uncommon; near 0 = close to the global average. The overview also shows which specific answers drove the score up or down.

Can I compare against my age group, gender, or country?

Yes. The overview has filters for Everyone, your gender, your age range, age + gender combined, or your country — so you can see how you compare to a peer group that actually looks like you.

How accurate are the comparisons?

Every percentage is calculated from real anonymous responses. As more people answer, the comparisons get more representative. Segments with fewer than 30 responses are flagged as low confidence.

How is Comparizzon different from a personality test?

Personality tests put you in a box with a type or letter code. Comparizzon doesn't — it shows, question by question, how your actual answers compare to everyone else's. Real, statistical context for your life choices, no labels.
